\begin{abstract}
Working abstract This paper leverages census data to investigate the
intricate relationship between individuals reported ancestry and their
self-identified racial classification on the United States Census.
Analyzing a dataset drawn from U.S. census records spanning 2010-2020,
this research employs statistical techniques to discern patterns,
trends, and discrepancies in how individuals perceive their ancestral
heritage and racial identity.
\end{abstract}
